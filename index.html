Interested in Joining the Co-Op?

MRIC is structured as a Co-Op. Members aren't just customers - they are a part of our internet community. MRIC is not out to make a profit; our members come first.

Member Benefits Include:

Voting Rights - have a voice

Knowlege that you are supporting your local community

Internet speeds average between 60-100mbps for upgraded areas

Coverage and Costs

Due to the nature of mountain access points, contact us directly to find out if you area is covered.

Cost

$60/month

$50 one-time membership fee

If equipment is needed: $50 installation fee pluss the cost of equipment (normally $100-300)
